"Why not go to a school near your house, like I do myself"
Police chief - City Police Commissioner Seemant Kumar Singh - said choosing neighbourhood schools could help ease traffic. “Opting for a school that takes two to three hours of commute in a school bus is a choice. People need to be more cognisant of this and instead opt for a school much closer to home."

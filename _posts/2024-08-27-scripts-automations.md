### **Basic Customizations and tweaks and set-up**

- **Tasker Automations**: Various automations, such as turning off Wi-Fi/Bluetooth when leaving home and turning it back on when returning
- **Keyboard Switcher**: Switch to Hacker's Keyboard when opening Terminal or specific apps
- **WhatsApp Auto-Reply**: Automatically reply to selected WhatsApp contacts using LLMs
- **Voice-to-Text Notes**: One-click voice-to-text note-taking with a single button press
- **Location Sharing**: Automatically send location to L when she sends a special message
- **Spam Report**: 1-click email report for last spam call/text received
- **Notes Sync**: Automatically sync notes between phone and laptop (Syncthing, rsync, rclone)
- **Reddit Redirect**: Automatically open old.reddit.com link when opening a reddit.com post thread



### Computer Scripts and Tools
